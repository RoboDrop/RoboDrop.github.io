About

Neil Wilson is a lab automation specialist with a passion for creating efficient, scalable solutions for life science labs. With over 15 years of experience in molecular biology and a deep understanding of automation, Neil has helped labs streamline their processes, improve accuracy, and scale operations.

Drawing on years of hands-on experience in both academic and industry settings, Neil focuses on delivering tailored automation strategies that meet the unique needs of each lab. His approach is grounded in collaboration, innovation, and a commitment to empowering labs to work smarter and faster.
